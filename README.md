# Netflix Content Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Netflix's content catalog to understand content distribution, genre trends, release patterns, country-wise content production, and business insights using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

The objective is to identify content trends and provide data-driven recommendations that can support content acquisition and platform growth strategies.

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains information about Netflix movies and TV shows, including:

* Title
* Type (Movie / TV Show)
* Director
* Cast
* Country
* Date Added
* Release Year
* Rating
* Duration
* Genre

## Key Insights

* Movies constitute a larger share of Netflix's catalog compared to TV Shows.
* Content production increased significantly after 2015.
* A small number of countries contribute the majority of Netflix content.
* Drama and International genres dominate the platform.
* Recent years show a strong focus on global content expansion.

## Business Recommendations

* Continue investing in high-performing genres.
* Expand content acquisition in emerging markets.
* Increase localization strategies for international audiences.
* Strengthen investments in regions showing growing content demand.
